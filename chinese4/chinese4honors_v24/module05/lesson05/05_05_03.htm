写汉字 Writing Chinese Characters

Before starting your actual writing, let’s look at the demonstration of how to write some new Chinese characters that you just learned, related to regions of China, stroke by stroke. You will use these characters and any others that you’ve previously learned for your writing later in this lesson.

Note: You can also practice writing these characters, using a printable worksheet. This worksheet has areas for you to practice writing the characters, stroke by stroke. Feel free to print several copies for extra practice if you want. You can practice them on your own if you are interested.
