我的宣传册 My Pamphlet

Now it’s time to put theory and thoughts into action! For this lesson, you will create a short portion of a pamphlet for one of the regions of China you have learned.

Before creating your pamphlet, look at a in China for a tourism location called Paradise Island. This sample is to show you what the portion of a pamphlet in Chinese looks like in real life, and you are not required to study or comprehend it. You will not be assessed on its content. The portion of pamphlet you are creating in this lesson should be similar to this sample but simpler, and you will be using the vocabulary and grammar you have learned.


Scenario

Let's pretend that you are a member of a tourism company in China. Your first assignment for your job is to create a pamphlet advertising a region of China. Luckly for you, last week you were reviewing information about the Yunnan Province, the Fujian Province, and the Inner Mongolia autonomous region. You will only have to pick one of the regions to highlight in your pamphlet. In your pamphlet, please include: 1) the region selected, 2) a description of the geography or weather from the region selected, and 3) a description of the culture or ethnic group from the region selected. Also 4) you can research online for information about this region to support your advertising, and you will provide the citation including the resource(s) you use for your research.

Use the " Template" for your work. You can also type your own portion of the pamphlet in Chinese using other formats.

You can use the characters demonstrated in this lesson and/or any other characters you’ve learned previously. You can choose to type the characters directly into a word document, and then save the document as your work.

Notes

  • If you are using the template provided in the lesson, the content needed for each part of your writing is indicated within the parentheses on the template. As you create your writing with the template, you can remove the preexisting text within the parentheses as needed.
  • Be sure to type the characters in your work instead of copying and pasting them from the lesson. You are encouraged to handwrite the characters when possible. It is the best way to understand the characters.
  • The information you use for this activity can be fictitious. It doesn't have to be real.
  • Be sure to include at least one resource you use in your research, following the APA/MLA citation guidelines.

You will use this to help with your work. Enjoy the activity!

What to include in your pamphlet Hint

Sentence #1

Identify the region selected and why one should go there.

Think about:

In this sentence, you may use grammar structures such as 要是 yàoshì + situation, 就 jiù + action in a Chinese sentence to express "if…then." You may also use other grammar structures you have learned.

Here is an example to help you get started. (The example may have some new words and is just for your reference. Your work should be in your own words, using what’s taught in this module.)

天堂岛是我们地区最大的海岛。你要是喜欢海滩，就应该来这里度假。

The Paradise Island is the largest sea island in our region. If you like beaches, then you should come here for vacation.

Sentence #2

Describe the weather or geography in the region.

Think about:

In this sentence, you may use grammar structures such as 即使 jíshǐ + situation, 也 yě + action in a Chinese sentence to express "even if…still." You may also use other grammar structures you have learned.

Here is an example to help you get started. (The example may have some new words and is just for your reference. Your work should be in your own words, using what’s taught in this module.)

天堂岛一年四季都很暖和，即使是在冬天，人们也只需要穿衬衫短裤。

It’s warm in Paradise Island all year round, and even if it’s winter time, people still just need to wear shirts and shorts.

Sentence #3

Describe the local culture or ethnic group(s).

Think about:

In this sentence, you may use grammar structures such as 不是 bùshì + possibility 1 + 就是 jiùshì + possibility 2 to intensify an adjective or emphasize a verb and express "either…or." You may also use other grammar structures you have learned.

Here is an example to help you get started. (The example may have some new words and is just for your reference. Your work should be in your own words, using what’s taught in this module.)

天堂岛的本地居民不是畲族就是土家族。他们几百年来一直和睦相处。

The local residents on the Paradise Island belong to either the She ethnic group or the Tujia ethnic group. They’ve been living in harmony for the past few hundred years.
