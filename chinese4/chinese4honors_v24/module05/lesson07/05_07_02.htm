渔猎生活 Fishing and Hunting

Describing how people make a living is an important and interesting topic when studying a region. It's good to know how to discuss different trades or livelihoods. Let's learn some related vocabulary words.

Note: Pay attention to each character as you may use them in the future. Refer to the character stroke sequence if you want to review how a character taught in this lesson is written.

Select the image to see the word or phrase. Listen to the audio, and then practice by recording yourself pronouncing what you hear. Practice as many times as you need to sound like a native speaker!

Notes

  • In the word 打猎 dǎliè (to hunt), the first character, 打 dǎ, means "to hit" as a verb, and the second character, 猎 liè, by itself, means "to hunt," with which you can form new words, such as 猎人 lièrén (hunter) and 猎物 lièwù (prey/quarry/game).
  • In the word 农业 nóngyè (agriculture), the first character, 农 nóng, by itself, means "agriculture," and the second character, 业 yè, means "line of business/occupation," with which you can form new words, such as 渔业 yúyè (fishery), 牧业 mùyè (animal husbandry), and 工业 gōngyè (industry). You may also have learned words with the character 业 yè, such as 作业 zuòyè (homework) and 事业 shìyè (career).
  • In the word 捕鱼 bǔyú (to catch fish), the first character, 捕 bǔ, means "to catch," and the second character, 鱼 yú, means "fish."

It's Also Said

  • In both words 农民 nóngmín (farmer) and 渔民 yúmín (fisherman), the last character, 民 mín, can be replaced with the character 夫 fū with the same meanings: 农夫 nóngfū (farmer) and 渔夫 yúfū (fisherman).
  • The word 捕鱼 bǔyú (to catch fish) is also said as 打鱼 dǎyú.

Vocabulary in Action

Let's look at how the new vocabulary words you just learned are used in Chinese sentences.

Note: Besides the vocabulary words you are learning in this lesson, you may see some unfamiliar words in the example sentences. Remember that you will not be assessed on these unfamiliar words.

我爸爸经常去森林里打猎。

My dad often goes hunting in the forest.

这个地区的农业很发达。

The agriculture in this region is very developed.

中国东北的农民都很勤劳。

Farmers in Northeast China are all very hardworking.

我舅舅住在海边，他每天都出海捕鱼。

My uncle lives by the ocean, and he ships out to catch fish every day.

这条河边住着很多渔民。

Many fishermen live along this river.
