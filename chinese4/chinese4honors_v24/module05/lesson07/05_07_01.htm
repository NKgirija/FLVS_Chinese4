你去过中国的大东北吗？东北三省有着悠久的历史和文化。那里也有广阔的土地和丰富的自然资源。

Have you been to the great Northeastern China? The Three Northeast Provinces have a long-standing history and culture. There are also vast lands and rich natural resources there.

China has a very diverse culture and landscape throughout its vast land. The Northeast China is one of the most distinctive regions. It has been known as the industrial empire of the nation since the 1930s, and its vibrant local culture and lifestyle have attracted tourists from around the world. Let's learn about this magical region together, using the Chinese language. Have fun!

阅读威力 Reading Power

Let's look at a written paragraph from David, a high school student, about his trip to China with his friends last winter. Select the orange globe to view the meaning in English.

去年冬天我和朋友们一起去了中国的东北旅行。那里的冬天很冷。我每次出门以前，都得穿好靴子和外套，再戴上手套和帽子。中国东北有很多著名的历史建筑，像博物馆、教堂、清真寺、火车站等等，又古老又美丽。我们还去滑雪了。那里许多人都很擅长滑雪。中国的东北真是个好地方 ！

Last winter my friends and I went to Northeast China for a trip. Winters there were very cold. Each time I went out, I had to put on my boots and coat and wear my gloves and hat. There were many famous historic buildings in Northeast China, such as museums, churches, mosques, train stations, and so on, which were both ancient and beautiful. We also went skiing. Many people there were very good at skiing. Northeast China is truly a great place!
