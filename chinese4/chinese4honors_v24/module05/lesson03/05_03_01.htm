  2. Know the requirements of your voice activity.

    For this voice activity, please record an audio or video pretending you are describing the Yunnan Province or the Fujian Province to a friend or family member. You will use complete sentences in Chinese. Be sure to use only the vocabulary from this course. Your voice activity should include the following.

    • Describe a weather feature in the region of your choice. In this sentence, you may use the grammar structures 就算 jiùsuàn + situation, 也 yě + action to express "even if…still." You may also use other grammar structures you have learned so far. (The sample provided is in English, but your work should be in your own words and in Chinese.)

      The Guizhou Province is rainy. People often say that even if you stay there for three months, you still can't see a sunny day.

    • Describe the culture in the region of your choice. In this sentence, you may use the grammar structure 不是 bùshì + possibility 1 + 就是 jiùshì + possibility 2 to express "either…or." You may also use other grammar structures you have learned so far. (The sample provided is in English, but your work should be in your own words and in Chinese.)

      People living in the mountains in the Guizhou Province either hunt or farm.

    • Describe an exotic animal in the region of your choice. In this sentence, you may use the grammar structures 只要 zhǐyào + condition, 就 jiù + result to express "as long as." You may also use other grammar structures you have learned so far. (The sample provided is in English, but your work should be in your own words and in Chinese.)

      There are many wild monkeys in Guizhou. As long as you walk into the mountains, you will most likely see monkeys.

Note: When providing responses to the questions, you can use made-up information if you choose not to share your own. Just make sure to use a variety of vocabulary to showcase your knowledge and skills.
