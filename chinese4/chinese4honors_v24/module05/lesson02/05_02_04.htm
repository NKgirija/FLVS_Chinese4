描述天气 Describing Weather

When we describe a place, it's important to know how to describe the local weather. For example, you can tell your friend what the temperature is in a particular region or how humid or dry it is there. Let's learn some vocabulary words you can use for such topics.

Note: Pay attention to each character as you may use them in the future. Refer to the character stroke sequence if you want to review how a character taught in this lesson is written.

Notes

  • In the word 极端 jíduān (extreme), the first character, 极 jí, means "utmost," and the last character, 端 duān, means "end(noun)."
  • In the word 热度 rèdù (heat), the first character, 热 rè, means "hot," and the second character, 度 dù, means "degree." The character 度 dù (degree) is also in the word 温度 wēndù (temperature). It can be combined with other words to describe the degree of a certain attribute. For example, you can use 湿度 shīdù (humidity) and 强度 qiángdù (strength). The word 热度 rèdù (heat) can also be used to describe the popularity of a public figure. For example, 这个明星的热度很高。Zhègè míngxīng de rèdù hěn gāo. (This celebrity's popularity is very high.)

It's Also Said

  • The word 温度 wēndù (temperature) is also said as 气温 qìwēn when it's used to describe the temperature in the air.
  • The word 热度 rèdù (heat) is also said as 热力 rèlì or 热量 rèliàng. Another word with a similar meaning is 热浪 rèlàng (heat wave).

Vocabulary in Action

Let's look at how the new vocabulary words you just learned are used in Chinese sentences.

Note: Besides the vocabulary words you are learning in this lesson, you may see some unfamiliar words in the example sentences. Remember that you will not be assessed on these unfamiliar words.

这个地区属于热带气候。

The climate in this region is tropical.

台风是一种极端天气。

A typhoon is a type of extreme weather.

福建省的气候很温和。

The climate in the Fujian Province is very mild.

今天下午的温度有点高。

The temperature this afternoon is a little high.

沙漠上的热度可以致命。

The heat in the desert can be lethal.
