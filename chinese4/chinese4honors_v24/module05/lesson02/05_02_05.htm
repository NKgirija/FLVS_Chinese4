Now let’s look at some grammar structures that you can use for this lesson.

Expressing the only two possibilities "either…or" using "不是 bùshì…就是 jiùshì"

You can use the structure 不是 bùshì + possibility 1 + 就是 jiùshì + possibility 2 to express "either…or."

Note: You may see some unfamiliar words in the example sentences. Remember that you will not be assessed on these words in this lesson. Just focus on the grammar points you are learning.

For example:

她戴的首饰不是金的就是银的。Tā dài de shǒushì bùshì jīnde jiùshì yínde.(The jewelry she wears is either gold or silver.)

这个夏天她不是去云南就是去福建。Zhègè xiàtiān tā bùshī qù Yúnnán jiùshì qù Fújiàn. (This summer she either goes to Yunnan or to Fujian.)

今天晚上不是下雨就是下冰雹。Jīntiān wǎnshàng bùshì xiàyǔ jiùshì xià bīngbáo. (This evening it'll either rain or hail.)
