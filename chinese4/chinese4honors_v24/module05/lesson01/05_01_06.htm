Now let's put things together and see how the Chinese language works in our friend David's everyday life! This time, David visited a remote village in southwestern China, and when he returned, he wrote about his experience.

这个地区很热也很潮湿，有许多绿色的树林。这里的人们说的语言和我的不同，不过他们穿的衣服和我的很相似。我还看到这里的人们都喜欢戴银色的首饰，格外漂亮。

This region is very hot and very humid, and there are many green woods. The language that people here speak is different from that of mine; however, the clothes they wear are very similar to mine. I also see that people here all like to wear silver jewelry, which is especially pretty.
