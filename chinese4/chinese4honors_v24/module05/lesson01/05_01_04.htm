谈论文化 Talking about Culture

When you visit a place, it's essential to get to know the local culture. Do you know how to describe different aspects of a culture to someone in Chinese? Let's learn some vocabulary words in Chinese related to the topic.

Note: Pay attention to each character as you may use them in the future. Refer to the character stroke sequence if you want to review how a character taught in this lesson is written.

Notes

  • In the word 语言 yǔyán (language), both the first character, 语 yǔ, and the last character, 言 yán, mean "speech/language," while 语 yǔ can be used to formally identify specific languages. It needs to be placed at the end of the word. For example: 汉语 hànyǔ (Chinese), 英语 yīngyǔ (English), and 西班牙语 xībānyáyǔ (Spanish) are placed at the end of the word. Sometimes in informal settings, you can use the word 话 huà to identify a language. For example: 中国话 Zhōngguóhuà (Chinese). For the last character, 言 yán, you can use it to form new words. For example, use 方言 fāngyán (dialect).
  • The antonym (word with opposite meaning) for the word 不同 bùtóng (different) is 相同 xiāngtóng (same).
  • In the word 首饰 shǒushi (jewelry), the first character, 首 shǒu, means "head" in formal settings, and the last character, 饰 shì, means "to decorate/decoration."
  • With the word 银 yín (silver), you can form new words such as 银色的 yínsède (silver color). It can also be used in an idiom such as 穿金戴银 chuānjīndàiyín (wearing gold and silver).

It's Also Said

  • The word 相似 xiāngsì (similar) is also said as 类似 lèisì or 相像 xiāngxiàng.
  • The word 首饰 shǒushì (jewelry) is also said as 珠宝 zhūbǎo.

Vocabulary in Action

Let's look at how the new vocabulary words you just learned are used in Chinese sentences.

Note: Besides the vocabulary words you are learning in this lesson, you may see some unfamiliar words in the example sentences. Remember that you will not be assessed on these unfamiliar words.

她很喜欢学习新语言。

She likes to learn new languages.

这两个地区的文化很相似。

The cultures in these two regions are very similar.

他喜欢研究不同地区的气候。

He likes to research the climates in different regions.

我妈妈参加聚会的时候会戴首饰。

My mom wears jewelry when attending parties.

这个杯子是用银做的，很珍贵。

This cup is made of silver, and it's very precious.
