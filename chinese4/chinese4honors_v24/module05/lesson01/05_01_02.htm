谈论各地区 Talking about Regions

When sharing your experience visiting a place, it's important to know how to describe the region so you can describe it to your family and friends when you return home. Let's learn some vocabulary related to regions.

Notes

  • In the word 人们 rénmen (people), the first character, 人 rén, means "person," and the last character, 们 men, is the suffix to be placed after a noun or pronoun to make it plural. You can form new words with the first character, 人 rén, (person). For example, you can use 人口 rénkǒu (population), 熟人 shúrén (acquaintance), and idioms such as 人山人海 rénshānrénhǎi (huge crowds of people). For the last character, 们 men, as a suffix, you can place it after a pronoun or sometimes a noun to make them plural as well. For example, you can say 我们 wǒmen (we/us), 你们 nǐmen (you (plural)), and 他们 tāmen (they).
  • In the word 地震 dìzhèn (earthquake), the first character, 地 dì, means "earth," and the last character, 震 zhèn, means "to shock." The word 地震 dìzhèn (earthquake) can also be used as a verb meaning "have an earthquake."
  • In the word 潮湿 cháoshī (humid), the first character, 潮 cháo, by itself, means "damp," and the last character, 湿 shī, means "wet." The first character, 潮 cháo, when used as a noun, means "tide."
  • In the word 干燥 gānzào (dry), the first character, 干 gān, means "dry," and the last character, 燥 zào, means "arid/parched." The first character, 干 gān, can be used by itself as a noun to refer to dried food items. For example, you can say 牛肉干 niúròugān (beef jerky) and 芒果干 mángguǒgān (dried mango).
  • In the word 许多 xǔduō (many), the second character, 多 duō, by itself, means "many." It can be used before a weather phenomenon to describe a weather condition. For example, you can use 多云 duōyún (cloudy) and 多雨 duōyǔ (rainy).

It's Also Said

The word 人们 rénmen (people) is also said as 人民 rénmín, 民众 mínzhòng, or 百姓 bǎixìng.

Vocabulary in Action

Let's look at how the new vocabulary words you just learned are used in Chinese sentences.

Note: Besides the vocabulary words you are learning in this lesson, you may see some unfamiliar words in the example sentences. Remember that you will not be assessed on these unfamiliar words.

这里的人们都很善良。

People here are all very kind.

这个地区经常发生地震。

Earthquakes often happen in this region.

中国的东南部气候很潮湿。

The climate in southeastern China is very humid.

这个夏天特别干燥，两个月都没有下雨。

It's very dry this summer, and it hasn't rained for two months.

她很喜欢旅行，去过许多国家。

She likes to travel, and she's been to many countries.
