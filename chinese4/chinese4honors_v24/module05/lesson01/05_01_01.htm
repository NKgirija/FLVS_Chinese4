准备好了吗？Ready to Go?

你喜欢旅行吗？你有没有去过中国的西南？那里的云南省特别美丽，很值得去看看。

Do you like to travel? Have you been to southwestern China? The Yunnan Province is especially beautiful, and it is very worth going for a look.

We live in a naturally and culturally diverse world. Through traveling, we get to experience such diversities and enjoy the beauty of local landscape, weather, and culture. This is especially true for the Yunnan Province in southwestern China, where its amiable climate, beautiful nature, and varied ethnic groups are well-known and much appreciated throughout the nation and in the world. Together, let's explore this beautiful province in every detail, using the Chinese language. Have fun!

阅读威力 Reading Power

Let's look at a written paragraph from David, a high school student, about a family trip in the past. Select the orange globe to view the meaning in English.

去年秋天我们一家人去了中国的西南旅行。那里的天气挺暖和的，一点也不冷。我每天都穿衬衫和牛仔裤。我们去的乡村有山，有湖，有树林，还有农舍。我们还看见了很多动物，像猴子、兔子、山羊和鸭子等。那里的人对我们都很好，还给了我们很多帮助。

Last fall, my whole family traveled to southwestern China. The weather there was quite warm, and it was not cold at all. I wore a shirt and jeans every day. The village where we went had mountains, lakes, woods, and farmhouses. We also saw many animals, such as monkeys, rabbits, goats, and ducks. People there were very nice to us, and they gave us a lot of help.
