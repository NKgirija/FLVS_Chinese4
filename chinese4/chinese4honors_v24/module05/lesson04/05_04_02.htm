美丽的土地 The Beautiful Land

There are different types of lands in this world, such as forests, wetlands, prairies, deserts, etc. Let's learn some vocabulary words so you can discuss related topics in Chinese.

Note: Pay attention to each character as you may use them in the future. Refer to the character stroke sequence if you want to review how a character taught in this lesson is written.

Select the image to see the word or phrase. Listen to the audio, and then practice by recording yourself pronouncing what you hear. Practice as many times as you need to sound like a native speaker!

Notes

  • In the word 草原 cǎoyuán (prairie), the first character, 草 cǎo, means "grass," and the last character, 原 yuán, means "original."
  • In the word 沙漠 shāmò (desert), the first character, 沙 shā, means "sand," and the last character, 漠 mò, by itself, means "desert."
  • In the word 平坦 píngtǎn (flat), both characters 平 píng and 坦 tǎn, by themselves, mean "flat." The character 平 píng from 平坦 píngtǎn (flat) and the character 原 yuán from 草原 cǎoyuán (prairie) can be combined as a new word 平原 píngyuán (plain).
  • In Mongolian regions, the special 帐篷 zhàngpeng (tent) that people live in has its own name, 蒙古包 Ménggǔ bāo (yurt), in which the first two characters, 蒙古 ménggǔ, mean "Mongolia," and the last character, 包 bāo, means "bundle/bag" as a noun and "to wrap" as a verb. You can form new words with the character 包 bāo, such as 面包 miànbāo (bread) and 包子 bāozi (steamed stuffed bun).

Vocabulary in Action

Let's look at how the new vocabulary words you just learned are used in Chinese sentences.

Note: Besides the vocabulary words you are learning in this lesson, you may see some unfamiliar words in the example sentences. Remember that you will not be assessed on these unfamiliar words.

这张照片上的草原太美丽了！

The prairie in this picture is too beautiful!

在中国的北部有沙漠。

There are deserts in Northern China.

我哥哥喜欢去森林里探险。

My older brother likes to have adventures in the forest.

我们去露营的时候搭了一个帐篷。

We set up a tent when going camping.

从我家到你家的这条路很平坦。去公园拍视频。

The road from my house to yours is very flat.
