Now let's put things together and see how Chinese language works in our friend David's everyday life! This time David visited the Inner Mongolia region in Northern China, and when he returned, he wrote about his experience.

上个月我去了中国的内蒙古自治区。在那里我看到了美丽的草原和森林。那里的草原很平坦，森林里有很多矿产资源。我没有去北边的沙漠，因为那里太干燥了。

Last month, I went to the Inner Mongolia autonomous region in China. There I saw beautiful prairies and forests. The prairies there are very flat, and there are many mineral resources in the forests. I didn’t go to the desert in the north because it’s too dry there.
