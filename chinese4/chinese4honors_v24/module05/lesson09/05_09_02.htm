Caution: Don't get locked out!

When completing the exam, it is important to pay attention and not get kicked out. Here are some situations that can lock you out, lose all your hard work, and give you grief:

If you are not working consistently on the exam, your internet service provider may kick you offline. This means you would lose all your work and have to start over. Your instructor will have to reset the exam for you if this happens.

  • Stepping away or working inconsistently on the exam may kick you offline. If you lose all your work and have to start over, be sure to contact your instructor for support with resetting your exam.
  • You cannot open the exam, take your dog for a walk, and then come back. Leaving the exam, even to complete it on paper, will count as a submission—even if there aren't any answers.
  • You cannot minimize your browser or work in more than one browser at a time during the exam.
  • Opening any other parts of the course while taking the exam will get you locked out.
